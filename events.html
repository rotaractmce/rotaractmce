REFLECTIONS



Reflections is a platform for all the young minds of Hassan to portray their talent and their creativity. It is a place where we witness the magic of young minds!! Reflections in carried out for 14 long years and will continue to do so. It is an attempt to unravel the talent and creativity of little souls. We host the event for students in and around Hassan and witness a spectrum of young kids whose talents are flawlessly crafted. Team spirit to prevail over one's own stardom and the overall championship is upheld. Reflections championship trophy was bagged by schools like Hassan Public School, Podar InternationalSchool, Netaji Public school etc. The scholarship is also rewarded for the most deserved. Participants with a wide aura of talent are encouraged here, as this event is a combination of cultural, literary, science, art, and creative spheres We contrived Sparshaartha, as a part of Reflections The event is for visually challenged students of Blind school, Hassan. This event is our humble effort to value the special talents in them by providing a platform. This event boosts the enthusiasm of specially born children and makes them feel strong and confident.

TUSSLE AND HUSSLE


TUSSLE

Tussle a mock placement event. An opportunity to experience an anticipated placements. The students actively participate in aptitude, group discussions, and interview sessions. This event strengthens leadership and entrepreneurial abilities. This event strengthens leadership and entrepreneurial abilities. Interview will be conducted by real HR as the performance of the candidate the marks will be provided by the HR. And the result will be according to performance of the candidate in these three steps. The winner is awarded with cash prize.


HUSSLE

Hussle is a fun activity based group event. It was newly introduced in 2023. There will be different rounds in the event. All the students of different years actively participate in this event every year. The main aim of this newly invented HUSSLE event was to bring confidence, spirituality and sportsmanship among the students. The winners are awarded with cash prize.


SAVISKAAR


Saviskaar is an event for freshers who have just joined the college. It is usually conducted by two clubs combined. It is a platform for first years to showcase their talents and mingle with the new environment. Saviskaar comprises of both cultural and technical events almost containing more than 20 events. To make the event more interesting a championship trophy is given branch wise. The branch with the highest points wins the trophy.

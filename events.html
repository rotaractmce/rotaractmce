REFLECTIONS

Reflections is a platform for all the young minds of Hassan to portray their talent and their creativity. It is a place where we witness the magic of young minds!! Reflections in carried out for 14 long years and will continue to do so. It is an attempt to unravel the talent and creativity of little souls. We host the event for students in and around Hassan and witness a spectrum of young kids whose talents are flawlessly crafted. Team spirit to prevail over one's own stardom and the overall championship is upheld. Reflections championship trophy was bagged by schools like Hassan Public School, Podar InternationalSchool, Netaji Public school etc. The scholarship is also rewarded for the most deserved. Participants with a wide aura of talent are encouraged here, as this event is a combination of cultural, literary, science, art, and creative spheres. We contrived Sparshaartha, as a part of Reflections. The event is for visually challenged students of Blind school, Hassan. This event is our humble effort to value the special talents in them by providing a platform. This event boosts the enthusiasm of specially born children and makes them feel strong and confident.

TUSSLE AND HUSSLE

TUSSLE

Tussle is a mock placement event. An opportunity to experience an anticipated placement. The students actively participate in aptitude, group discussions, and interview sessions. This event strengthens leadership and entrepreneurial abilities. Interview will be conducted by real HR; the performance of the candidate is evaluated, and marks are provided by the HR. The result will be according to the performance of the candidate in these three steps. The winner is awarded a cash prize.

HUSSLE

Hussle is a fun activity-based group event. Introduced in 2023, it involves different rounds. Students from various years actively participate every year. The main aim is to bring confidence, spirituality, and sportsmanship among students. Winners are awarded a cash prize.

SAVISKAAR

Saviskaar is an event for freshers who have just joined the college. Conducted by two clubs combined, it provides a platform for first-year students to showcase their talents and adjust to the new environment. The event includes cultural and technical events, with more than 20 activities. A championship trophy is awarded branch-wise. The branch with the highest points wins the trophy.
